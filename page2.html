My education

I went to primary school at OU ,,Goce Delcev"-Tetovo. I finished every year with perfect grades and then when it was time to think about my future (in which High School was i going to go) i had a big dilemma.

Knowing that the High School in Tetovo had fallen a long way from where it had been in terms of teachers and education, i decided to go to ,,Rade Jovcevski-Korcagin" It was a big challenge, but i managed to finish all my years with perfect grades, and that gave me the boost to go on and continue my university education at FINKI.
